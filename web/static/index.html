The pipeline

Four steps from idea to story.

01

Classify

We read your prompt and identify the story's genre — adventure, friendship, fantasy, and more — to select the right tone and pacing.

02

Plan

Before a single word of prose is written, a structured story arc is created: setup, conflict, rising action, climax, and a warm resolution.

03

Write

A specialized storyteller agent writes the full prose — rich in sensory detail, natural dialogue, and age-appropriate vocabulary.

04

Review

An independent editor agent scores the story on six dimensions. If it falls short, the story is revised and re-evaluated automatically.
